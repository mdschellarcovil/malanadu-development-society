About Us

Local dairy and agriculture leadership since 1994

Serving Chellarcovil, Idukki and surrounding communities with fresh milk, Malanadu products, and support for 67 local farmers.

Our story

Founded in 1994, Malanadu Development Society is a farmer-driven cooperative built to preserve traditional dairy practices and bring fresh, traceable products directly to the community.

We collect milk from local producers, craft premium dairy and agricultural goods, and distribute them under a trusted local brand.

Established1994

What we do

Milk collection

Daily collection from village farmers, ensuring fresh dairy reaches consumers within hours.

Local markets

Direct distribution in Chellarcovil and nearby towns for farm-fresh milk and goods.

Premium products

Pure ghee, coconut oil, spices, honey and tea crafted with traditional care.

Community impact

Empowering farmers with fair value and supporting sustainable agriculture in the region.
