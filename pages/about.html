About Us

Premium dairy leadership from Chellarcovil

Established in 1994, Malanadu Development Society combines local farmer partnerships with premium product delivery across Kerala.

Our cooperative legacy

Malanadu Development Society was founded in 1994 in Chellarcovil, Idukki to support local dairy farmers and deliver fresh, trustworthy products to the community.

We collect milk directly from local producers, process it with care, and bring a refined set of dairy and Malanadu goods to customers across the region.

Today, our cooperative stands for sustainable sourcing, transparent local milk sales, and a portfolio of premium quality products made in Kerala.
