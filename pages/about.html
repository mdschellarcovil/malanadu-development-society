Our story

Trusted dairy cooperative with local reach

Our cooperative promise

  • Fresh milk from nearby farmers
  • Premium coconut oil, ghee, honey, tea and coffee
  • Professional local sales and distribution
  • Strong commitment to Chellarcovil and Idukki
